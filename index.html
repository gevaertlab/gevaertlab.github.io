About the Lab

The Gevaert Lab focuses on developing novel multimodal AI methods for complex diseases, with a particular focus on oncology and cardiovascular disease. We develop novel machine learning approaches that digest multi-omics, multimodal, and multi-scale data.

Our work spans Bayesian and kernel methods, regularized regression, and deep learning to integrate molecular data and omics. We also investigate linking omics data with cellular and tissue data in the context of computational pathology, imaging genomics, and radiogenomics.

A central goal is the development of a framework for non-invasive personalized medicine — building what we call a medical digital twin that integrates multi-scale patient data to support clinical decisions.
