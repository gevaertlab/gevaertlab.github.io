Join Us

We are recruiting graduate students, postdoctoral scholars, and visiting scientists who are passionate about multimodal AI for precision medicine. We particularly welcome applicants with backgrounds in machine learning, computational biology, medical imaging, or bioinformatics.

If you are interested in joining the lab, please reach out via the Stanford faculty profile above with a brief description of your background and research interests.
