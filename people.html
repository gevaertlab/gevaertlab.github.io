OG

Principal Investigator

Olivier Gevaert

Associate Professor of Medicine and Biomedical Data Science

Dr. Olivier Gevaert is an associate professor at Stanford University focusing on machine-learning methods for biomedical decision support from multi-scale data. He trained as an electrical engineer with additional expertise in artificial intelligence, and holds a PhD in bioinformatics from the University of Leuven, Belgium. He completed his postdoc in radiology at Stanford before establishing his lab in the Department of Medicine.

Affiliated faculty at IBIIS. Member of BioX, Stanford Cancer Institute, Cardiovascular Institute, Wu Tsai Neurosciences Institute, and MCHRI.
