Principal Investigator

OG

Olivier Gevaert

Associate Professor of Medicine and Biomedical Data Science · Stanford University

Dr. Olivier Gevaert is an associate professor at Stanford University focusing on developing machine-learning methods for biomedical decision support from multi-scale data. He is an electrical engineer by training with additional training in artificial intelligence, and holds a PhD in bioinformatics from the University of Leuven, Belgium. He completed his postdoc in radiology at Stanford before establishing his lab in the Department of Medicine in Biomedical Informatics.

The Gevaert Lab focuses on multi-scale biomedical data fusion primarily in oncology and neuroscience, developing machine learning methods including Bayesian, kernel methods, regularized regression and deep learning to integrate molecular data and omics — and linking omics data with cellular and tissue data in computational pathology and imaging genomics.

Affiliated faculty at IBIIS. Member of BioX, Stanford Cancer Institute, Cardiovascular Institute, Wu Tsai Neurosciences Institute, and MCHRI.
